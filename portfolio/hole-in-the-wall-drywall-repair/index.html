Hole in the Wall Drywall Repair

 

 

 

Logo and Branding for a startup local drywall repair company based in Orlando. The client provided the name, and I dialed in a rugged, distressed type style and earthy color scheme geared toward a home-improvement aesthetic that resulted in one happy customer and some pretty quick business for him. For this client in particular, I also did some strategy brainstorming and helped him push upfront pricing. It turned out to be a successful combination for him: a decent logo paired with upfront pricing.
